About StudentStay

Our Mission

StudentStay was founded with the mission to make student accommodation search easy, safe and transparent. We understand the challenges students face when moving to a new city for education.

Our platform connects students with verified accommodation providers and helps them find the perfect place to stay near their college or university.

Why Choose Us?

  • Verified listings with real photos
  • Gender-specific filters for safety
  • Detailed information about facilities
  • Honest reviews from fellow students
  • 24/7 customer support
